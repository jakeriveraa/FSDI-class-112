Spaghetti Bolognese

Classic Italian spaghetti with a rich and savory meat sauce.

Ingredients:
  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion & garlic
  • Herbs & spices
Instructions:

Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. In a pan, sauté onions and garlic, add ground beef until browned, then mix in tomato sauce and herbs. Serve sauce over spaghetti.

Fluffy Pancakes

Soft, fluffy pancakes perfect for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
Instructions:

Mix all ingredients into a smooth batter. Heat a pan and pour small portions to cook pancakes until golden on both sides. Serve with syrup or fresh fruit.

Chocolate Cake

Moist and decadent chocolate cake, perfect for celebrations.

Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
Instructions:

Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Mix dry ingredients, then add eggs and butter. Pour into a greased pan and bake for 30-35 minutes. Cool and enjoy!
